# PolyCode SQL Certificate — Requirements

Earn the **PolyCode SQL Developer Certificate** by completing this course and demonstrating practical skills.

## Requirements

### 1. Complete all modules

| Module | Lessons |
|--------|---------|
| 01 Beginner | 10 |
| 02 Intermediate | 6 |
| 03 Advanced | 7 |
| 04 Professional | 6 |
| 05 Mastery | 4 |

### 2. Hands-on projects

Complete **at least 3 projects** across the course (console app, structured program, capstone).

### 3. Final assessment

- Score **70% or higher** on the [final assessment](02-final-assessment.md)
- Review weak topics before retaking

## How to submit

Track progress in your PolyCode profile and complete the assessment when ready.
